To you,

I know my sorry isn’t enough to clear my mistakes… I know I lost your trust, and with that, your respect. And I understand why you became cold.

Still, I want to apologize wholeheartedly. I am truly sorry, and I really love you.

I know you’re tired of my bullshit, tired of everything repeating, tired of getting hurt again. I know you don’t want to go through all of this one more time.

I know you’ve been crying. I know you’ve been heartbroken. I know you’ve been losing sleep.

I’m not asking you to forget what I did, and I’m not asking you to pretend it didn’t matter. But if we ever tried to truly understand each other one more time, I believe we could fix things… without erasing the past.

This place changed me in ways I didn’t realize. It isolated me, it messed with my head. I won’t use it as an excuse for my actions, but you know this place hasn’t been good for me either.

I’m staying out more now. My mind is slowly gaining clarity. I see things differently than before.

I know you don’t owe me anything. I know you might never trust me the same way again. But I needed you to know that I’m willing to change, not just say it.

I still think about our good times, not to escape my mistakes, but to remember what we once were.

Whatever you decide, I’ll accept it. I just wanted you to hear this from me… honestly.

— always yours in truth
